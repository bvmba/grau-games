# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 4, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GRAU Arcade ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Discord Activity.

## Information We Collect

### Information from Discord

When you use our Activity, we receive the following information from Discord:
- **User ID**: Your unique Discord identifier
- **Display Name**: Your Discord username or display name
- **Avatar**: Your Discord profile picture URL

### Game Data

We collect and store the following game-related data:
- Game scores and statistics
- Coins earned through gameplay
- Number of games played

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Display your profile within the Activity
- Track your game progress and scores
- Show leaderboards to all users
- Improve the gaming experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers and is associated with your Discord User ID. We retain your data for as long as you use the Activity or until you request deletion.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:
- When required by law
- To display public leaderboards (username, avatar, and scores only)

## Leaderboards

By using this Activity, you acknowledge that your display name, avatar, and game scores may be visible to other users through the leaderboard feature.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of leaderboard visibility (contact us)

## Children's Privacy

This Activity is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, please contact us through Discord or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
